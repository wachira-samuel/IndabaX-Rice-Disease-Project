Causes

Brown spot is caused by the fungus Bipolaris oryzae. It is prevalent in areas with poor soil fertility and inadequate water management.

Signs and Symptoms

  • Small, circular brown lesions on leaves and grains.
  • Lesions may have a yellow halo and can coalesce, leading to larger dead areas.
  • Infected grains may be discolored and shriveled.

Treatment

Management includes improving soil fertility, ensuring proper irrigation, and using fungicides when necessary.

Recommendations

  • Use resistant rice varieties adapted to local conditions.
  • Apply balanced fertilizers to improve plant health.
  • Practice crop rotation to reduce disease pressure.
